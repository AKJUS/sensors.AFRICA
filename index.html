THE AFRICAN HUMAN DEVELOPMENT SERVICE

Helping you find the data you need.

Takwimu is a new African digital information service launching in 2018 to provide high quality data and analysis for players in the human development space, focusing on ten countries in Africa: Nigeria, Senegal, Burkina Faso, Zambia, South Africa, Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Ethiopia, and DRC Congo.
